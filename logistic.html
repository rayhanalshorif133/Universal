Commercial Cleaning

Commercial Cleaning Services That Exceed Your Standards

A Commercial Cleaning Company That Provides Exceptional Cleaning from Floor to Ceiling

A clean environment creates an essential first impression and contributes to the health and safety, morale, and productivity of your workforce. Whether you need office cleaning services, commercial window cleaning, or industrial kitchen cleaning services, Proactive Cleaners provides commercial cleaning services in London, Oxford, and across the southeast, and we carry out our work to an exceptionally high standard.

We hold a wide range of accreditations, including ISO 9001, 14001, and 45001, in addition to PASMA and IPAF for high-level and confined space cleaning, ensuring that we are fully equipped to meticulously clean your establishment from floor to ceiling.

We pride ourselves on providing comprehensive cleaning services for many different industries and organisations.

Our range of cleaning services includes the following:

Contract Cleaning Services

Have your communal spaces regularly cleaned with our contract cleaning services. Please contact us to get a no-obligation, bespoke quote.

Business and Office Cleaning Services

Ensure your business makes a great first impression on clients and visitors while creating a comfortable, safe, productive, and welcoming environment for your team.

Retail, Leisure and Hospitality Cleaning

Customers have high expectations, so it is vital to maintain high levels of cleanliness to keep your customers feeling confident in your services. That’s why the work of specialised commercial cleaning companies is so important.

Education Environments

It’s imperative to maintain an educational setting safely and hygienically. Our commercial cleaning services can take on this challenge and ensure you meet the necessary safety standards and requirements.

Events and One-Off Cleans

We know that every event presents individual and unique challenges. We can provide cleaning services for any sort of event, from corporate hospitality to music festivals.

Carpet Cleaning Services

With state-of-the-art carpet machines and eco-friendly non-hazardous solutions, we can eradicate stains, dirt, and dust to give all carpets a new lease of life.

Commercial Floor Cleaning

We can maintain your walk spaces to a high standard and complete a thorough clean of many different types of flooring materials to ensure that your flooring is safe and free of dirt and debris, creating a lasting impression for staff, customers and visitors. We can efficiently clean all types of floors, including hardwood, tile, concrete, carpet, vinyl plank, laminates, linoleum, and more.
